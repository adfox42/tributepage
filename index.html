Kurt Vonnegut Tribute Page

November 11th, 1922 — April 11th, 2007.
Best selling novel: Cat's Cradle
My favorite quote: “Here we are, trapped in the amber of the moment. There is no why.” — Kurt Vonnegut.

Kurt Vonnegut was the first author whose work I absolutely fell in love with. I remember going to my local bookstore and purchasing Slaughterhouse-Five.
I finished the novel in a day. From there I went on to purchase more of his novels and quickly became obsessed with his work. My favorite novel that he
wrote was "Mother Night".
